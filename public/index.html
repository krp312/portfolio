Hello kind passerby! I'm Kris, and I'm a web developer. Born in L.A. and raised in New Jersey.

I used to be a chemist, but I switched to programming because (1) I think it's fascinating, (2) and I like making useful things for people I love.

My logo is a waveform because I'm also a musician, and lover of music in general. I can easily enjoy a sweaty DIY concert just as much as the Philadelphia Orchestra.

I'm a lifelong Knicks fan and it has been rough! I also love to cook. Currently I'm exploring curries. And I like to wonder about mundane things taken for granted, like how our road traffic laws came to be, or how advertising affects our decision making.
